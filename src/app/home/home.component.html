Customer Portal
The Smart Customer Portal is a cloud-based, lightweight portal built on SAP Cloud Platform allowing customers to retrieve key information such as account information, ticket information, orders, deliveries and invoices. The portal is custom-built for a specific business and can be further personalized as needed.
Vendor Portal
A vendor portal is generally an internet-based system used to manage and connect with third party suppliers of goods or/and services. It should be a secured management system adopted by organizations that network with multiple suppliers. Vendor Portals, also known as Supplier Portals.
Employee Portal
Employee Portal is a feature-rich solution that allows employees and field staff to see the SAP Business One data they need in a web-based browser. This portal is to be designed for providing info about the organization and employees, getting the pay slip as printable, leave balance and to request leave.
